Key dates & actions
Five decisions over the next 5 years
Month 13
Start Balanced Advantage fund SWP
Only after 12 full months does the fund qualify for LTCG (12.5%) vs STCG (20%). Starting one month early costs ₹10–15k extra tax. Do not start in Year 1.
Year 5 — SCSS
Decide: extend SCSS or redeploy?
If SCSS rate at renewal is ≥7%, extend for 3 more years. If it falls to 5–6%, move into RBI FRSB or AAA target-maturity bond funds.
Year 6 — SGB
Redeploy SGB proceeds: 40% Debt + 60% Hybrid
SGB matures tax-free. Redeploy to restore the plan's growth engine: 40% into debt buffer, 60% into Balanced Advantage.
Every April
File ITR — declare SWP as capital gains
SWP = redemption of units = capital gains event. Must be reported in Schedule CG even at ₹0 tax. Use ITR-2.
Equity ↓ 15%+
Pause hybrid SWP, use debt buffer
Never sell equity at a loss. Pause Balanced Advantage SWP via your AMC's portal (takes 2 min). Draw from debt fund. Restart when markets recover.
Capital preservation rule: Interest alone (~₹26k/mo net) covers most of Year 1. SWP from growth funds covers the rest. Follow the waterfall order and pause equity SWP during corrections — corpus stays stable in the base case.
Withdrawal strategy
Choose which products to draw SWP from, in what proportion, for each year. The interest income always comes first — this only controls how the SWP gap is filled.
How it works: Your fixed interest (SCSS + RBI bond + SGB) arrives automatically each month. The remaining gap to hit your net target is the SWP. You decide which product(s) to withdraw from each year.

Tax tip: Arbitrage funds = equity tax (LTCG/STCG) but low risk — ideal for early years. After 1 year they qualify for LTCG 12.5% (vs short-term 20%). Pull from these first, then switch to Balanced Advantage / hybrid for later years.
